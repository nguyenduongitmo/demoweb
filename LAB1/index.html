Discover Russia: cities, nature & culture

Experience Russia’s beauty — from the bustling streets of Moscow to the calm waters of Lake Baikal.

Top Destinations

  1. Moscow: The capital city, full of history and modern life.
  2. St. Petersburg: Home to the majestic Hermitage Museum.
  3. Lake Baikal: The world’s deepest freshwater lake, perfect for nature lovers.

Suggested 7-day Itinerary

  • Day 1–3: Explore Moscow
  • Day 4–5: Visit St. Petersburg
  • Day 6–7: Relax at Lake Baikal
